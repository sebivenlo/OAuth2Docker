The following sums up the motivation and goal of our OAuth2 workshop. It shall describe that users
may learn during our workshop and how we plan to reach those learning goals.

Learning goals:

- Let students know about OAuth2, especially the reason and main concept
- Let students know about real life use cases of OAuth2 
- Let students know about OAuth2 in a practical approach by letting them 
  access an OAuth2 server to authenticate a certain app
- Let students know about the archictecture of the previously mentioned app
  and explain what just happened in the background
